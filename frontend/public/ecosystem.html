🌱 Ecosystem Simulation

Explore how animals, plants, and humans interact in a dynamic ecosystem. Adjust human impact, weather, and simulation speed to see how changes affect the balance of nature.

Controls & Information

In this simulation, key natural processes have been fixed to reflect realistic ecological behavior:

  • Prey behaviors: Prey animals (gazelles, rabbits) have a set range to detect food and a fixed speed for evasion.
  • Plant growth: Grass regrows is affected by weather conditions.
  • Predator-prey dynamics: Predators (lions) hunt prey, and these interactions drive the ecosystem balance.
  • Human Impact: Higher human impact increases pollution and deforestation, reducing overall health.
